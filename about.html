About Us

Our Mission

AIxponential is driven by the belief that artificial intelligence has the power to transform our world for the better. Our mission is to ensure that this transformation is guided by the principles of truth, knowledge, access, and trust. We strive to:

  • Empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to understand and utilize AI effectively.
  • Promote the development and deployment of ethical and unbiased AI systems.
  • Expand access to AI education and resources for all, regardless of background or location.
  • Foster a collaborative environment where knowledge is shared, and the future of AI is shaped by a diverse and informed community.

Our Team

AIxponential is led by a passionate team of experts in AI, education, and non-profit management. We are currently building our board of directors and advisory boards. Check back soon to meet our team!

Our Guiding Principles

  • Truth: We are committed to accuracy, transparency, and unbiased information.
  • Knowledge: We believe in the power of education to empower individuals and communities.
  • Access: We strive to make AI knowledge and resources accessible to all.
  • Trust: We build trust through ethical practices and responsible AI development.
  • Hope: We envision a future where AI is a force for good, creating a more equitable and prosperous world.
  • Collaboration: Working across partners, volunteers, and staff.
