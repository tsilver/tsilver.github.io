We are in the early days of AI. My friends and I were there for the early days of the PC, early days of the internet, early days of mobile, early days of cloud computing, and now we are in the early days of AI. We are here to help you on the journey the future of AI.

Our Mission

To democratize AI education, making it accessible, engaging, and ethical for everyone, regardless of background or resources. We believe that AI literacy is crucial for navigating the future, and we're committed to empowering the next generation of innovators, thinkers, and responsible AI users.

Our Initiatives

tröe: Impartial and Transparent Tools for AI Engineering

Pursuing practical AI Engineering insights to support AI Engineering decision making. We are transparent in our methodology and continuously incorporate feedback to remove bias. tröe leverages volunteer services to develops open source tools to conduct public and private experiments. Check out the work on Content Moderation intended to inform stochastic decision making.

AI Summer Camp: Igniting Young Minds

Our AI Summer Camp is an immersive experience designed to spark a passion for AI in the next generation through hands-on projects, interactive workshops, and engaging challenges.
