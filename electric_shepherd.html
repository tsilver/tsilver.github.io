Our Mission

To democratize AI education, making it accessible, engaging, and ethical for everyone, regardless of background or resources. We believe that AI literacy is crucial for navigating the future, and we're committed to empowering the next generation of innovators, thinkers, and responsible AI users.

Our Initiatives

Empowering Educators: Building the Future of AI Literacy

Join us in shaping the next generation of AI innovators. Our "Teach the Teacher" initiative provides educators with the tools, resources, and support they need to confidently bring AI education into the classroom. We believe that empowering teachers is the key to unlocking the transformative potential of AI for all students. We are committed to providing a comprehensive curriculum that is adaptable to existing educational frameworks in both STEM and the Humanities. We leverage a community of volunteers and promote an open-source model to encourage collaboration and feedback. The foundation of our curriculum is based on understanding the core concepts of AI literacy and how to use large language models to help create and deliver curriculum suited to a wide variety of students. Our goal is to incorporate AI into as many aspects of education as possible so that students will gain familiarity and develop the intuition needed to effectively interact with AI on a daily basis.

Co-Creating the Future: AI Curriculum Development for Educators

Be a part of building a cutting-edge AI curriculum. Our "Teach the Teacher" program is focused on collaborative development, empowering educators to contribute to and shape the future of AI education. We provide the framework, tools, and support; you bring your expertise and classroom experience to help us create engaging and relevant learning experiences for students. Our focus is on creating a core curriculum that can be adapted to specific needs and to showcase how AI tools can be incorporated into the classroom to improve efficiency and learning. Our AI tools use a structured approach to curriculum development that encourages broad participation and rapid development.

Unlocking the Power of AI: Mastering Prompt Engineering for the Classroom

Prompt engineering is the key to harnessing the full potential of AI. Our "Teach the Teacher" program provides educators with in-depth training on prompt engineering, empowering them to guide students in effectively interacting with large language models and other AI tools. Join us and learn how to craft prompts that inspire critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving. We help teachers develop their own skills at creating effective prompts and also create a framework for incorporating prompt engineering into every aspect of the curriculum. Our approach to curriculum development is designed to make prompt engineering a core skill that is used to enhance learning in every subject.

Developing Human Skills: AI to support learning

The key to effective use of AI is developing strong human skills for working with AI. Our "Teach the Teacher" program helps educators learn how to use AI to evaluate what underlying skills are needed to use AI tools and to incorporate those into the curriculum. We help educators create curriculum that uses AI to enhance education and improve learning outcomes for students. We focus on fundamental skills and how AI can be leveraged to learn new skills and to support traditional education.
