Get Involved: Power the AI Revolution Through Education and Openness

AIxponential is built on the belief that the transformative power of AI should be accessible to everyone. We're a community-driven organization, and your contributions are vital to achieving our mission of democratizing AI knowledge and fostering responsible innovation. Join us in building a future where AI empowers individuals and communities.

Ways to Contribute

1. Donate Compute Resources: Fuel AI Education and Transparency

Help us power the engine of AI education and research! We're seeking contributions of compute resources to run our educational programs and support our unbiased LLM analysis.

  • Non-Invasive Approach: Run a local client with full control over your machine and data
  • Configurable Options: Align with your preferences and available resources
  • Transparency & Openness: All data available under Creative Commons license
  • Initial Focus: Support live educational events
  • Impact Tracking: Detailed reporting on usage and impact
  • Privacy First: You retain full control over your contribution

2. Contribute Your Software Development Expertise

We're seeking passionate software developers to help us build innovative tools and platforms that will power AI education, accessibility, and transparency.

  • Client Development: Browser extensions and local SLMs on Chromebooks
  • Prompt Evaluation: Analysis algorithms and moderation systems
  • Queue Management: Robust systems for managing prompt execution
  • Response Evaluation: Feedback generation and safety checks
  • UI Development: Engaging interfaces for students and instructors

3. Make a Financial Contribution

Your financial support is crucial to sustaining and expanding our programs. Donations fund tool development, operations, and program expansion. The founder has provided initial seed funding, and we actively seek grants and partnerships to amplify our impact.

Our Philosophy

AIxponential is inspired by the principles of open-source software, startup innovation, and decentralized organizations. We believe in the power of collaboration, transparency, and community ownership. We envision a future where diverse ideas and experiences blend synergistically to create an AI-powered world that benefits all.
