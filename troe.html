About tröe

tröe is a non-profit, open-source platform dedicated to providing unbiased, multi-perspective answers to complex questions. We are a community of engineers, researchers, and volunteers working together to create a more transparent and democratic approach to accessing knowledge.

tröe conducts tests and provides metrics to inform decision making regarding the diversity of AI systems. As a not for profit, the organization is intended to be a clearing house of open and transparent information, tools, and process for use in AI engineering. We encourage feedback and We believe in the power of open-source AI and community-driven efforts to make technology more reliable and trustworthy.

Our Services

tröe offers a suite of services designed to evaluate and benchmark AI systems, with a focus on content moderation as a primary use case.

Standardized Benchmarking

Evaluate your AI models against our curated datasets of e-commerce comments, labeled for objectionable content.

API-Driven Evaluation

Periodic Testing

How It Works

1. Submit Your AI

Use our API to submit your content moderation model or system for evaluation.

2. Automated Evaluation

tröe runs your AI against our standardized datasets or your custom scenarios.

3. Receive Detailed Reports

Get comprehensive performance data, including precision, recall, F1-score, latency, and error analysis.

4. Publish or Keep Private

Choose to make your results public to contribute to open knowledge or keep them private for internal use.
